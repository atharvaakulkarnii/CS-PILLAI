FOUNDING
TEAM

Here is the Founding-team: the directors, founders, and promoters who govern the company's affairs on a day-to-day basis.

VISIONARY

He is the beating heart of the team. While everyone in the company is on realizing the overall goals and objectives, with the stars in the eyes, he organizes to see into the future and articulates that dream to the team and others. He convinces the skeptics among the customers, market, investors, and inspires the rest of the team. His job is, at all times, people-facing and he is all about the big picture. Whether it’s coming up with the overall strategy, recruiting and hiring the best talent for the company, or making sure that the right people, investors mainly, are kept happy. His eyes on the prize, he is always looking to capitalize on even the smallest of opportunities. He is always looking to grow and/or expanding the business and is ready to adapt and pivot if the circumstances call for it. Even if it’s through sheer force of character, no setbacks hold him back and barrel forward rallying the rest of the team as he goes. He organizes the rest of the team continually, and perhaps most importantly, inspires them.

PRODUCT PERSON

There is only one thing that he focuses on- product, product, product. The brains of the operation, his entire job is to make sure that the required tech and hardware is up to scratch. He is the one who knows the market and lives and breathes the product. From the get-go, he conceptualizes what the design of the product should be like—what people should expect and why it’s amazing. He is a problem-solver and always be looking for different ways to develop and improve the value of whatever he is working on.

FACILITATOR

If the visionary is the heart of the founding team, then she is the arms and legs. She is simultaneously the doer and the taskmaster of the group, setting the pace and making damn sure everyone else keeps up. What she doesn’t know she asks, and what can’t be efficiently done she’ll put in the hours to hammer it out. While she can see and understand the bigger picture, instead she focuses on the smaller scale of things. All those detailed bits and pieces, the day-to-day operations that need to be done in order for the grand vision to happen. The visionary may set up the board, but it’s she, hustler, that moves the pieces. She ultimately walks the middle road to fill whatever role is necessary until a more suitable replacement can be found. Bear in mind, that does not mean that the hustler is replaceable. She makes sure that the visionary and hacker stay grounded and working together. They keep their eyes on the bottom line and make sure the ship keeps driving forward.
